Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 50 developers in a local university auditorium. What started as a passion project for open-source collaboration has grown into one of the most respected technology conferences globally, attracting over 5,000 attendees annually.

Our Mission

To inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and accelerate technological progress by connecting brilliant minds from around the world. We believe in inclusive, accessible, and forward-thinking discussions that shape the future of technology.
